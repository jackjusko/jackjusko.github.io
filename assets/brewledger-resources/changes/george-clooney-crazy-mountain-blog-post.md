# Feature Analysis: George Clooney Crazy Mountain Blog Post

## Summary

Added **George Clooney's Crazy Mountain: The $1B Casamigos Playbook Hits Non-Alcoholic Beer** (`george-clooney-crazy-mountain-non-alcoholic-beer.md`) to both `server/content/blog/` and `platforms/console/src/blog/posts/`. The post covers the March 2026 launch of Crazy Mountain, a premium non-alcoholic lager from Clooney, Rande Gerber, and Mike Meldman—the Casamigos trio.

## Unique Spin

**The Casamigos playbook**: Rather than focusing on celebrity alone, the post analyzes how the same trio that sold Casamigos tequila for $1B is applying the same formula to NA beer—lifestyle brand, premium positioning, friendship-based authenticity, disciplined rollout. The production angle—**limit alcohol formation vs. strip it after brewing**—is highlighted as a craft-brewery-relevant technical choice that affects quality and operations.
