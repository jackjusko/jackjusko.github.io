## Content Overview

The post covers:
- **The turnaround:** Guinness's current winning streak vs. rumors of a Diageo spin-off nine years ago
- **The value of age:** Cultural currency, St. James's Gate, the accretion of meaning over centuries
- **People return to great brands:** How Sierra Nevada, Allagash, New Glarus—and Guinness—survived the craft novelty era
- **Why now:** Instability, young drinkers seeking safe ports, Guinness as both safe and different

Key takeaway: Diageo's decision to invest in heritage and new breweries rather than spin off Guinness is paying off. The post positions Guinness as a case study for American breweries entering their third, fourth, or fifth decade.
